More Than an Ultrasound

Everything You Need to Know Before You Visit

💛

2D Ultrasound

Our 2D ultrasound is a bonding experience, not a medical procedure. We don't perform medical exams such as transvaginal ultrasounds — this time is simply about meeting your baby.

👶

Gender Confirmation

Gender confirmation is possible from around 15 weeks. Depending on your baby's position and development on the day, we may not always be able to confirm it right away.

💳

Easy Payment

We accept cash and all major credit cards, so you can book and pay however is easiest for you and your family.
