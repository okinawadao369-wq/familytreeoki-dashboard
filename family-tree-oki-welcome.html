A Note From Our Founder

Hi, I'm Koya.

I grew up here in Okinawa, and I've spent my whole life around this island's relationship with the U.S. military community. My wife is a registered nurse, and together we started Family Tree Oki because we kept hearing the same thing from military families here: "We love it here, but when it comes to something as personal as pregnancy and family care, we don't always know who to trust."

That's the gap we wanted to fill. Family Tree Oki is a small, women-run studio built specifically for you — bilingual, familiar with military life, and focused on making sure you never have to figure this out alone.

— Koya Miyagi, Founder, Family Tree Oki

We never share your photos, videos, or personal information without your permission — full stop. Your session is between you and our team, unless you choose to share it yourself.

Honest Pricing

Why isn't this covered by TRICARE?

TRICARE covers medically necessary ultrasounds, but not elective sessions like gender reveal or keepsake imaging — that's actually part of why Family Tree Oki exists. We believe you deserve to know exactly what you're paying for before you walk in the door, with no surprise fees. We accept cash and all major credit cards, so you can book and pay however is easiest for your family.

Getting Here

First time off base? We've got you.
